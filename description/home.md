# Home

## Purpose

The Home screen is the place you begin every day.

Hermes intentionally avoids asking you to create a new to-do list every morning. Instead, it presents today's knowledge through **Today's Pursuit**, helping you begin with intention rather than deciding from scratch what to learn.

Everything shown on this page exists to answer one question:

> "What deserves my attention today?"

---

## Today's Pursuit

Today's Pursuit contains the knowledge scheduled specifically for the current day.

Items may be added manually, or automatically generated from your Manual Collections according to the Daily Limits you configured.

Today's Pursuit can contain:

- Questions
- Articles
- Notes
- Ideas
- Observations

Each item is intentionally limited so your day never becomes overwhelming.

Rather than showing everything you have collected, Hermes slowly surfaces knowledge over time.

---

## Starter Workspace

Every new installation includes a Starter Workspace.

The Starter Workspace exists to teach Hermes by example.

Inside it you'll discover every supported Item type together with their available actions and settings.

Nothing inside the Starter Workspace is permanent.

Once you understand the system, you are encouraged to create your own Workspace and remove the Starter Workspace entirely.

---

## Pinned Domains

Pinned Domains provide quick access to the areas of your life you visit most frequently.

Instead of searching every time, your most important Domains remain immediately available from the Home screen.

Examples:

• Engineering

• Mathematics

• Startup

• Philosophy

• Health

Pinning only changes visibility on the Home screen.

It does not affect the underlying organization.

---

## Pinned Blocks

Blocks represent focused subjects inside a Domain.

Pinning Blocks allows instant access to subjects you work on daily.

Example:

Engineering

↓

Python

↓

Pinned Block

This saves navigation while keeping the overall hierarchy clean.

---

## Recent Evolutios

Recent Evolutios display the latest pieces of knowledge that changed your understanding.

These are not simply recent notes.

An Evolutio represents a meaningful shift in your thinking.

Seeing them together provides a quick reminder of how your understanding is gradually evolving over time.

---

## Today's Veritas

Consistency is not always possible.

Sometimes life interrupts.

Veritas exists to record the truth rather than punish interruptions.

Whether today involved illness, travel, examinations, or rest, Hermes allows you to honestly document why your routine changed.

Knowledge grows best when reality is acknowledged rather than ignored.

---

## Workspace Menu

The current Workspace appears in the upper-right corner of the Home screen.

Selecting it opens Workspace Settings.

From here you can:

• View Archive

Browse archived items without mixing them into your active workspace.

• Visibility

Control which information appears on the Home screen.

• Lock Workspace

Protect the workspace using a PIN for private knowledge.

• Switch Workspace

Move between completely independent knowledge systems.

• Reload

Refresh the current workspace if changes were made elsewhere.

Its purpose is to quietly answer one question every morning:

"What deserves my attention today?"
