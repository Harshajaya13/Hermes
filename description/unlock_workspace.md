# Unlock Workspace

## Purpose

Protected Workspaces should remain secure while still being quick to access for their owner.

Unlock Workspace provides a simple authentication step before entering a protected Workspace.

Once the correct PIN is entered, Hermes immediately restores your complete learning environment exactly as you left it.

Security should never make your workflow frustrating.

---

## Why Unlocking Exists

Privacy is meaningful only when protection is consistent.

Without authentication, anyone holding your device could browse private journals, startup ideas, research notes, or personal reflections.

Workspace Unlock ensures that only someone who knows the correct PIN can enter a protected Workspace.

The process is intentionally simple.

One PIN.

One confirmation.

Your Workspace opens.

---

## Entering Your Workspace

After selecting a locked Workspace, Hermes requests your Workspace PIN.

If the PIN is correct:

- The Workspace unlocks.
- Your Home screen loads.
- Today's Pursuit is restored.
- Pinned Domains appear.
- Pinned Blocks return.
- Every Item becomes available exactly as you left it.

Nothing is recreated.

Nothing is reloaded from the internet.

Your Workspace simply becomes accessible again.

---

## Your Progress Is Preserved

Unlocking does not reset anything.

Everything remains exactly where you stopped working.

Your unfinished Questions remain.

Draft Notes stay intact.

Ideas continue evolving.

Observations remain connected.

Reflections stay editable.

Hermes remembers your progress so you can continue learning without interruption.

---

## Local Authentication

Workspace authentication happens entirely on your device.

No internet connection is required.

No cloud account is needed.

No personal information leaves your device.

Hermes believes your private knowledge should remain private.

Authentication simply confirms that the person opening the Workspace is its intended owner.

---

## A Calm Experience

Unlocking should feel invisible.

It exists only for the few seconds needed to verify access.

After that, Hermes fades into the background, allowing you to focus entirely on learning instead of security.

Good security protects your work.

Great security protects it without constantly reminding you that it exists.

---

## Philosophy

Privacy should never become friction.

Workspace Unlock exists to protect what matters while keeping your daily workflow smooth and uninterrupted.

Once you're inside, Hermes gets out of your way and lets you continue thinking, learning, and creating.
