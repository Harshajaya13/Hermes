# Switch Workspace

## Purpose

As your knowledge grows, you'll naturally create multiple Workspaces.

One for Engineering.

Another for your Startup.

Perhaps one for Research.

Another for Personal Journaling.

Switch Workspace allows you to move between these completely independent environments instantly while keeping every Workspace isolated and organized.

---

## Why Switching Exists

Your brain naturally changes context depending on what you're working on.

When studying Mathematics, you don't want Startup ideas competing for attention.

When designing your product, you don't want Operating Systems notes appearing everywhere.

Hermes respects these mental boundaries.

Switching Workspaces is like walking into a different room.

Everything unrelated stays behind.

Everything relevant comes with you.

---

## Completely Independent

Every Workspace maintains its own:

- Domains
- Blocks
- Questions
- Articles
- Notes
- Observations
- Ideas
- Reflections
- Evolutios
- Manual Collections
- Today's Pursuit
- Settings

Switching Workspaces never merges information.

Each Workspace remains its own self-contained knowledge system.

---

## Instant Context Switching

When you select another Workspace, Hermes immediately loads its entire environment.

The Home screen changes.

Pinned Domains change.

Pinned Blocks change.

Today's Pursuit changes.

Recent Evolutios change.

Everything you see now belongs only to the selected Workspace.

Nothing from the previous Workspace remains visible.

---

## Separate Daily Workflows

Every Workspace progresses independently.

For example:

Engineering

Today's Pursuit

• Solve Probability Question

• Read Linux Article

---

Startup

Today's Pursuit

• Review Product Idea

• Read Market Research

Even though both exist on the same device, Hermes keeps their learning journeys completely separate.

---

## Returning Later

Switching away from a Workspace never loses your progress.

Everything remains exactly where you left it.

When you return later:

- Your unfinished Questions remain.
- Draft Notes are preserved.
- Reflections stay editable.
- Today's Pursuit continues naturally.

Hermes remembers the state of every Workspace independently.

---

## Designed For Long-Term Learning

Many users eventually accumulate multiple areas of expertise.

Hermes was built with this in mind.

Instead of forcing every subject into one giant knowledge base, you can create focused environments that evolve independently over years.

Each Workspace grows alongside its own purpose.

---

## Privacy

If a Workspace is protected by a PIN, Hermes requests authentication before switching into it.

This allows sensitive or private Workspaces to remain protected while leaving everyday Workspaces easily accessible.

Privacy is handled at the Workspace level, giving you complete control over what remains protected.

---

## Philosophy

Learning requires focus.

Focus requires context.

Switching Workspaces isn't simply changing screens.

It's changing environments.

Hermes keeps every Workspace intentionally isolated so your attention stays on the work that matters right now.

When you change your Workspace, you change your entire learning environment.

Nothing more.

Nothing less.
