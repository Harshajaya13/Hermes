# Workspace Lock

## Purpose

Not every Workspace contains information you are comfortable sharing.

Some Workspaces may contain:

- Personal journals
- Startup ideas
- Research notes
- Private reflections
- Business plans
- Sensitive observations

Workspace Lock allows you to protect these environments with a PIN while leaving the rest of Hermes simple and accessible.

Privacy should be intentional, not inconvenient.

---

## Why Workspace Lock Exists

Knowledge is personal.

While some Workspaces are perfectly fine to share, others represent years of private thinking and should remain protected.

Instead of locking the entire application, Hermes allows protection at the Workspace level.

This means only the information that truly needs privacy is secured.

Everything else remains immediately accessible.

---

## How It Works

When Workspace Lock is enabled, Hermes requests your Workspace PIN before allowing access.

Without the correct PIN:

- The Workspace cannot be opened.
- Its contents remain hidden.
- Its knowledge stays protected.

This protection applies only to the selected Workspace.

Other Workspaces continue to function normally.

---

## Independent Security

Every Workspace manages its own protection.

For example:

Engineering

Unlocked

↓

Startup

Locked

↓

Personal Journal

Locked

↓

Research

Unlocked

This flexibility allows you to organize your knowledge without applying unnecessary security everywhere.

---

## What Is Protected?

When a Workspace is locked, everything inside it remains private.

This includes:

- Domains
- Blocks
- Questions
- Articles
- Notes
- Observations
- Ideas
- Reflections
- Evolutios
- Manual Collections
- Today's Pursuit

Nothing inside the Workspace becomes accessible until the correct PIN is entered.

---

## Everyday Workflow

Workspace Lock is designed to stay out of your way.

Most of the time, you'll continue working as usual.

Authentication only happens when entering a protected Workspace.

Once unlocked, your workflow remains exactly the same.

Hermes protects your knowledge without interrupting your learning.

---

## Local Privacy

Workspace PINs are stored locally on your device.

Your security never depends on cloud accounts or external authentication services.

Everything remains under your control.

Hermes protects your privacy without requiring you to surrender ownership of your knowledge.

---

## Peace of Mind

Many users eventually store years of work inside Hermes.

Workspace Lock provides confidence that personal thoughts, unfinished ideas, and sensitive projects remain available only to those who know the PIN.

Privacy should feel natural.

Not complicated.

---

## Philosophy

Knowledge is valuable because it is personal.

Hermes believes that privacy should exist where it matters most.

Workspace Lock protects individual knowledge environments while keeping the rest of the application fast, simple, and distraction-free.

Your thoughts belong to you.

Hermes simply helps you keep them that way.
