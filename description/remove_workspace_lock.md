# Remove Workspace Lock

## Purpose

Your security preferences may change over time.

A Workspace that once contained private research or personal reflections may eventually become something you're comfortable accessing without authentication.

Remove Workspace Lock allows you to disable PIN protection whenever you decide it is no longer necessary.

Security should always be your choice.

Never a permanent decision.

---

## Why Removing the Lock Exists

Privacy is not static.

As projects evolve and priorities change, some Workspaces naturally become less sensitive.

Perhaps:

- A startup has already launched.
- Research has been published.
- Personal notes are no longer private.
- A Workspace has become a shared knowledge base.

Rather than forcing permanent protection, Hermes allows every Workspace to evolve alongside its purpose.

---

## How It Works

Removing Workspace Lock requires authentication using the current Workspace PIN.

This ensures that only someone who already has legitimate access can disable the protection.

Once the correct PIN is verified:

- PIN protection is removed.
- The Workspace becomes immediately accessible.
- No knowledge is modified.
- No data is deleted.

Only the authentication requirement changes.

Everything else remains exactly the same.

---

## Your Knowledge Remains Safe

Removing a lock never affects your Workspace itself.

Your:

- Domains
- Blocks
- Questions
- Articles
- Notes
- Observations
- Ideas
- Reflections
- Evolutios
- Manual Collections

remain completely untouched.

Hermes only changes how the Workspace is accessed.

Never what it contains.

---

## Re-Enabling Protection

Removing a Workspace Lock is never permanent.

If your needs change later, you can enable PIN protection again at any time.

This flexibility allows Hermes to adapt to your workflow rather than forcing fixed security rules.

---

## Local Control

Workspace security is managed entirely on your own device.

No online account.

No password recovery service.

No cloud dependency.

You remain fully responsible for your own knowledge and its protection.

Hermes simply provides the tools.

The decisions remain yours.

---

## Designed For Flexibility

Some Workspaces deserve strong privacy.

Others are meant to be shared.

Hermes recognizes that there is no single security model that fits everyone.

Instead, it gives you complete control over every Workspace independently.

Your knowledge system should adapt to your life, not the other way around.

---

## Philosophy

Privacy is most valuable when it remains under your control.

Hermes never assumes how your knowledge should be protected.

Instead, it provides simple, transparent tools that allow you to choose when security is needed and when it is not.

Your Workspace.

Your decisions.

Your knowledge.
