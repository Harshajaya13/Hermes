# Search

## Purpose

Knowledge only becomes valuable when it can be rediscovered.

Most people remember learning something.

Very few remember exactly where they saved it.

Was it inside a folder?

A notebook?

A bookmark?

A browser tab?

A PDF?

Months later, finding that information often becomes harder than learning it again.

Hermes was designed to solve that problem.

Search allows you to find ideas rather than files.

---

## Why Search Exists

Human memory is associative.

You rarely remember where you stored something.

Instead, you remember fragments.

A keyword.

A concept.

A sentence.

A person's name.

A technology.

Hermes embraces this natural way of remembering.

Instead of forcing you to remember locations, Hermes helps you rediscover knowledge through meaning.

---

## Search Everything

Hermes searches across your entire workspace.

Results may include:

- Questions
- Articles
- Notes
- Observations
- Ideas
- Reflections
- Evolutios

You don't need to remember which Block contains the information.

Hermes searches the content itself.

---

## More Than File Names

Traditional search often depends on filenames.

Hermes searches much deeper.

It looks through:

- Titles
- Content
- Keywords
- Markdown
- Connected Items

This allows ideas to become discoverable even if you forgot exactly where they were written.

---

## Connected Knowledge

Because Items inside Hermes can be connected together, Search often reveals more than a single result.

For example:

Searching:

"SQLite"

may return:

- A Question
- An Article
- Several Notes
- An Observation
- A Reflection
- An Evolutio

Instead of isolated files, Hermes presents an entire network of related understanding.

---

## Local First

Search is completely local.

Nothing leaves your device.

There are no cloud servers indexing your knowledge.

Every search happens inside your own workspace.

This keeps searching fast while preserving complete ownership of your information.

---

## Growing With You

As your knowledge base expands, Search becomes increasingly valuable.

Hundreds of Notes.

Thousands of Questions.

Years of Articles.

Without an effective search system, this knowledge eventually becomes difficult to navigate.

Hermes ensures that growth never becomes clutter.

The larger your workspace becomes, the more important Search becomes.

---

## A Personal Knowledge Engine

Search is more than navigation.

It becomes a way of rediscovering ideas you had forgotten.

Many users will search for one topic and unexpectedly reconnect with observations, reflections, or ideas they recorded months earlier.

This creates new opportunities for learning and connection.

Sometimes the most valuable discovery is not something new.

It is something you already knew but had forgotten.

---

## Why This Workflow?

Knowledge should never disappear simply because time has passed.

Hermes believes that remembering ideas should feel effortless.

You should only need to remember **what** you were thinking about.

Hermes takes care of remembering **where** you wrote it.

Search transforms your workspace from a collection of files into a living library of your own understanding.
