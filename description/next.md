# Export

## Purpose

Knowledge should never be trapped inside an application.

You spent years building your understanding.

It should remain yours regardless of which operating system you use, which company built the software, or whether Hermes even exists decades from now.

Export exists to guarantee that ownership.

Your knowledge belongs to you.

Always.

---

## Why Export Exists

Many modern applications lock your information behind proprietary formats, subscriptions, or cloud services.

The day you leave the platform, your knowledge becomes difficult to access.

Hermes rejects that philosophy.

Everything you create should remain portable, readable, and completely under your control.

Export ensures that your work can always move with you.

---

## What Can Be Exported?

Hermes allows you to export different parts of your knowledge independently.

For example:

- Individual Blocks
- Entire Domains
- Complete Workspaces

Whether you want to archive years of research, migrate to another computer, or simply create a backup, Hermes makes the process straightforward.

---

## The `.hermes` Bundle

Hermes introduces its own portable bundle format.

A `.hermes` bundle contains everything required to recreate your exported knowledge exactly as it existed.

This includes:

- Questions
- Articles
- Notes
- Observations
- Ideas
- Reflections
- Evolutios
- Relationships
- Metadata

Nothing is lost during export.

Everything remains connected exactly as you organized it.

---

## Backup

Export is also your backup system.

Creating regular backups protects years of accumulated knowledge from unexpected hardware failures or accidental data loss.

Because your data is stored locally, backups are simple.

Copy the exported bundle wherever you trust.

Cloud storage.

External drives.

Personal servers.

The choice is entirely yours.

---

## Sharing Knowledge

Export is not only for backups.

It also makes sharing knowledge intentional.

You might create:

- A Probability Question Collection
- A Linux Learning Workspace
- A Deep Learning Research Library

Export it.

Share the bundle.

Someone else can import it into their own Hermes workspace while preserving the same structure you carefully created.

Knowledge becomes portable without sacrificing organization.

---

## Migration

Changing computers should never mean rebuilding your knowledge base.

Simply export your Workspace from one device and import it into another.

Within minutes, your entire environment is restored.

No manual copying.

No rebuilding.

No vendor lock-in.

---

## Future-Proof Ownership

Technology changes.

Applications evolve.

Companies disappear.

Hermes was designed with a different philosophy.

Your knowledge should survive regardless of what happens to the software itself.

Export protects that promise.

The application serves your knowledge.

Your knowledge should never serve the application.

---

## Philosophy

Hermes is not trying to own your ideas.

It exists to help you preserve them.

The greatest compliment Hermes can receive is that one day you carry your knowledge somewhere else because it has always belonged to you.

Freedom is not a feature.

It is a design principle.

Your knowledge begins with you.

It should always remain with you.
